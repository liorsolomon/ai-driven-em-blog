The AI Tools Every Engineering Manager Should Evaluate

Sep 12, 2025 · 9 min read

New AI tools for engineering teams ship every week, and most demos look incredible. The manager's job isn't to chase them — it's to figure out which ones survive contact with a real team. Use this four-question filter before you roll anything out.

Does it remove toil or add a tab?

A useful tool replaces work you're already doing. If it creates a new surface to check and maintain, the burden often outweighs the benefit.

Where does the data go?

Code, tickets, and 1:1 notes are sensitive. Understand the tool's data handling and retention before it touches anything proprietary.

Can you measure the before and after?

Adopt tools you can evaluate. Pick one metric — cycle time, review latency, on-call load — and compare a few weeks with and without.

Will the team actually use it?

Adoption beats capability. A modest tool people use beats a powerful one they route around.
